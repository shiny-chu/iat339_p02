NATURAL. SIMPLE. ORGANIC.

LooseLeaf use the most natural and organic ingredients to make the purest cup of tea that helps you relax and recharge through every sip.

Our Values

THE LEAVES ARE OUR CORE.

At LooseLeaf, we take pride in our premium quality tea leaves, so we never add any artificial flavour, colour or sweetener to our blends.

WE CARE ABOUT THE NATURE.

All our ingredients come from the nature, inspiring us to do everything we can to protect the planet. Therefore, we use minimal product packaging and use biodegradable materials whenever possible, so you can enjoy every cup of tea with confidence.

Our Story

Looseleaf Founder

Meet Jenny,

Growing up, I’ve always been obsessed with tea and how it can comfort yet energize you for the day. It probably had something to do with the memories of my parents bringing me a cup of tea whenever I needed comfort. As I grew more interested in teas, researched the benefits and tested out different blends, Looseleaf was born.

After discovering my passion for tea, I travelled across Asia and lived in Taiwan for two years to learn more. After this experience, my goals for this company was to introduce delicious and unique blends of tea to tea addicts like me. Looseleaf has all the qualities that are important to me: comfort, sustainablility, quality at a affordable price, and pretty packaging.

I hope you will enjoy what Looseleaf has to offer!

- Jenny
